# RAAS (Renin Angiotensin Aldosterone System)

- Has 4 organs
- `Liver` produce **Angiotensinogen** protein
- ↓ Renal blood flow or ↓ blood volume & pressure
  - → `Kidney` produces **Renin**
  - → **Renin** converts **Angiotensinogen** to **Angiotensin I**
  - → **ACE (Angiotensin-Converting-Enzyme)** converts **Angiotensin I** to **Angiotensin II** in `Lungs`
  - → Action of **Angiotensin II** (_vasoconstriction, thirst_)
  - → Release of **Aldosterone** from `Adrenal glands`
  - → Action of **Aldosterone** (_salt and water reabsorption_)
  - → ↑ Blood volume and pressure
- RAAS can be activated in hypertension when renal blood flow is decreased

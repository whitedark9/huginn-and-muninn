# Antihypertensive Drugs

## Classification

### 1. Diuretics

1. **Thiazide diuretics**
   - `hydrochlorothiazide`, `chlorthalidone`, `indapamide`

1. **Loop diuretics**
   - `furosemide`, `bumetanide`, `torsemide`

1. **Potassium-sparing diuretics**
   - `spironolactone`, `eplerenone`, `amiloride`, `triamterene`

### 2. Sympatholytic (Adrenergic Inhibitors)

1. **Centrally Acting Agents**
   - `clonidine`, `methyldopa`

1. **Ganglion Blockers** _(obsolete)_
   - `trimethaphan`, `hexamethonium`

1. **Adrenergic Neuron Blockers**
   - `reserpine`, `guanethidine`

1. **β-Adrenergic Blockers**
   - _Non-selective_ — `propranolol`, `nadolol`, `timolol`
   - _Cardioselective (β₁)_ — `atenolol`, `metoprolol`, `bisoprolol`, `nebivolol`
   - _With α-blocking activity_ — `labetalol`, `carvedilol`

1. **α-Adrenergic Blockers**
   - `prazosin`, `terazosin`, `doxazosin`

### 3. Vasodilators

1. **Direct Arteriolar Vasodilators**
   - `hydralazine`, `minoxidil`

1. **Parenteral Vasodilators (Emergency Use)**
   - `sodium nitroprusside`, `diazoxide`, `fenoldopam`

### 4. Calcium Channel Blockers (CCBs)

1. **Dihydropyridines**
   - `nifedipine`, `amlodipine`, `felodipine`, `nicardipine`

1. **Non-dihydropyridines**
   - `verapamil`, `diltiazem`

### 5. Drugs Acting on Renin–Angiotensin System (RAAS)

1. **ACE Inhibitors**
   - `captopril`, `enalapril`, `lisinopril`, `ramipril`

1. **Angiotensin II Receptor Blockers (ARBs)**
   - `losartan`, `valsartan`, `candesartan`, `irbesartan`

1. **Direct Renin Inhibitor**
   - `aliskiren`

### 6. Others / Miscellaneous

1. **Nitrates**
   - `nitroglycerin`, `isosorbide dinitrate`

1. **Dopamine agonist**
   - `fenoldopam` (also under vasodilators)
